How it works

BookReflect gives you a simple way to discuss books. Using BookReflect is easy. Take these easy steps to get started and join the discussion.

To get started...

  1. To create an account, click here and fill in your information in the fields.
  2. You will get an activation link at the email address you provided. Open the email and click on the link to activate your account.
  3. Login with your new username and password by clicking here or the Login button on top.

Once you have logged in

  1. Use the search bar on the home page to look for a book. You can put the title, the author, or even clues like a character's name or a name of a place where the story takes place.
  2. Select a book from the search results to see the short description and the related discussion topics.
  3. Select one of the existing discussion topics or create a new one.
  4. Leave your comment to let others see what you think.

Other ways to find books

  1. You can also find books by author or by genre by clicking the corresponding menu items.
  2. Popular authors and popular genres are also displayed on the right for quicker access.
